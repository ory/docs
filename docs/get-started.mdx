## New to Ory ?

- [Ory Projects Overview.](/ecosystem/projects.md) What is Ory ? Learn about the
  open source projects at the heart of Ory:
- [Ory Community Overview.](/ecosystem/community.md) A place where developers
  share and build: Learn more about the Ory Community:
- [Ory Kratos Quickstart.](https://www.ory.sh/docs/kratos/quickstart) User login
  and registration for web, mobile, native, or single-page applications.
- [Ory Keto Quickstart.](https://www.ory.sh/docs/keto/quickstart) Complex
  consistent permission and authorization management.
- [Ory Hydra Quickstart.](https://www.ory.sh/docs/hydra/5min-tutorial)
  Authenticate third-party users and secure access to your applications and
  APIs.
- [Ory Oathkeeper Quickstart.](https://www.ory.sh/docs/oathkeeper/configure-deploy)
  Authenticate, authorize and mutate any incoming network traffic, with a Zero
  Trust security model.

Don't want to deal with Docker, Kubernetes, and securing your API?

  Ory Cloud. ([Learn more.](/cloud/developer.mdx))
