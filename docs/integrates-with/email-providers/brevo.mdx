[Brevo](https://www.brevo.com) (formerly Sendinblue) is a European email platform with a strong GDPR posture. Use it when you need
EU-hosted email infrastructure.

See [Sending emails over SMTP](../../kratos/emails-sms/01_sending-emails-smtp.mdx#brevo) for the full configuration.
