[ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com) — B2B data intelligence platform.
Enrich Ory identities post-registration with B2B contact + company
data so newly registered users land pre-qualified in your funnel.

## How it works

Ory Action on `registration.after` (async) → handler authenticates
(OAuth2 client credentials) → calls
`POST https://api.zoominfo.com/enrich/contact` → PATCHes returned data
to `metadata_public.zoominfo` on the identity.

## Notable

- **B2B-focused** — personal-email signups won't resolve. Gate calls
  on a business-email check.
- US business contact data is strongest; EU coverage is weaker and
  subject to GDPR opt-outs.
- Licensing is the primary constraint.
