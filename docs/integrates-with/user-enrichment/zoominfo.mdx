[ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com) is a B2B data intelligence platform.
You enrich Ory identities after registration with B2B contact and company
data so newly registered users land pre-qualified in your funnel.

## How it works

An Ory Action on `registration.after` runs asynchronously and calls your handler, which
authenticates with OAuth2 client credentials, calls
`POST https://api.zoominfo.com/enrich/contact`, and PATCHes the returned data
to `metadata_public.zoominfo` on the identity.

## Notable

- **B2B-focused:** personal-email sign-ups won't resolve, so gate calls
  on a business-email check.
- US business contact data is strongest. EU coverage is weaker and
  subject to GDPR opt-outs.
- Licensing is the primary constraint.
