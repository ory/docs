Google is configured as a social sign-in provider in the Ory Console or via the Ory CLI — no webhook code required. See

Add Google as a social sign-in provider in Ory Network. Google is a widely used OIDC provider: users sign in with their existing
Google account (consumer Gmail or Google Workspace) and Ory maps the returned claims to the identity schema.
