Add Sign in with Apple as a social sign-in provider in Ory Network. Apple uses a privacy-focused OAuth2 flow with Hide My Email
relay addresses and requires JWT-signed client assertions (Apple Team ID + Private Key) rather than a plain client secret.
Required for iOS apps that offer any third-party social login per App Store guidelines.
