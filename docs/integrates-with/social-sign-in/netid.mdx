Add NetID as a social sign-in provider in Ory Network. NetID is the European single sign-in alliance backed by Mediahuis, RTL, and
ProSiebenSat.1 — common for German and EU consumer media products that want a privacy-respecting, GDPR-aligned sign-in option.
