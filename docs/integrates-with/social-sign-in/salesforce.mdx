Salesforce is configured as a social sign-in provider in the Ory Console or via the Ory CLI — no webhook code required. See
[Add Salesforce as a social sign-in provider in Ory](../../kratos/social-signin/42_salesforce.mdx) for the full guide.
</Admonition>

Add Salesforce as a social sign-in provider in Ory Network. Useful for B2B products integrated into the Salesforce ecosystem —
sales reps and admins sign in with their existing Salesforce account, and the access token can be reused to call Salesforce APIs.
