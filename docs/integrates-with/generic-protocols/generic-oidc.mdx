Any OIDC-compliant Identity Provider is configured into Ory Polis as the OIDC Relying Party — no custom code required. See
[Set up app in your OpenID Connect Identity Provider](../../polis/sso-providers/generic-oidc.mdx) for the full guide.
</Admonition>

Configure any OpenID Connect-compliant Identity Provider with Ory. For B2B SSO with per-organization isolation, Ory Polis acts as
the OIDC Relying Party on behalf of an organization. For consumer or user-facing sign-in, Ory Identities (Kratos) provides
federated sign-in.
