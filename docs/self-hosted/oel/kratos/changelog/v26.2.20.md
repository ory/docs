## v26.2.20

### Notify previous addresses on self-service email change

Ory Kratos now supports notifying an identity's previous email address when they change their email address through self-service
flows.

If the `notify_previous_addresses` hook is configured, the user will receive a notification at their previous email address
informing them of the change. This notification includes information about the change and instructions on how to secure their
account if they did not authorize the change.

This change includes a new email template for the notification, which can be customized to fit the branding and tone of your
application. The template includes placeholders for the user's name, the new email address, and a link to secure their account if
they did not authorize the change.
